Description

Simple arcade style game. Shoot down all the asteroids.

But be careful! If an asteroid hits your ship you lose!

Score points vary depending on the type of asteroid you destroy.

Your fire rate increases as you get more points!

Sounds too easy? You lose score points for any asteroid you miss!
