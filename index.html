Description

Simple arcade style game. Shoot down all the asteroids.

But be careful! If an asteroid hits your ship you lose!

Score points vary depending on the type of asteroid you destroy.

Your fire rate increases as you get more points!

Sounds too easy? You lose score points for any asteroid you miss!

This game is designed for arcade game lovers who wants more of a challenge and improved graphics

Game Controls

This game is designed to have intuitive controls. This means that you will naturally know what controls to use

But here is a quick description of them.

Use W,A,S,D or the arrow keys to move. Press space-bar or the left mouse click to shoot.
